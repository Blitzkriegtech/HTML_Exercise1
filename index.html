The king of the Jungle


Lion (Panthera leo), large, powerfully buit cat (family Felidae)
that is second in size only to the tiger; it is a famous apex predator.

The proverbial "King of beasts", the lion has been one of the best-known
wild animals.

General Characteristics

Lions are a well-muscled cat with a long body, large head, and short legs.
Size and appearance vary considerably between the sexes.
